Welcome to Fashionkilla!

Fashionkilla is a website that allows you to save and keep track of all your clothes in one easy-to-access online environment.
Think of it as a digital closet for your on-the-go shopping, where you might need some help remembering what clothes you already own or what store-bought clothes could potentially go well with clothes you already own.
See below for usage instructions and navigation.

Navigation:

At the top of the screen are five buttons that will redirect you to four different pages:
Regsiter, Login, My Closet, Explore, and Outfits.


Register:

Click "Register" at the top of the page to make a Fashionkilla account.
Afterwards, you may Log In.


Login:

By logging in to an account, Fashionkilla can save and store your changes so that you may come back to your closet anytime, just as you left it.
It also allows you to see other users' outfits online via the "Explore" page.


My Closet:

The "My Closet" feature will likely be of greatest interest to you. After logging in, the My Closet page will allow you to add articles of clothing to your closet.
When doing so, you can give your article of clothing a name, upload an image of it, and assign it different tags like Clothing Type and Color(s). The clothing type and color tags you assign to your clothes will help you to oranize them and find them more easily in the future, as well as find other clothes that might match their color schemes.
By having several different clothing types, you may also begin to craft outfits, which Fashionkilla allows you to do via the Outfits feature.


Outfits:

The "Outfits" function allows you to randomly generate an outfit from all of the articles of clothing in your closet.
Results will be a compilation of seperate randomly-selected clothing types from a pool of each clothing article of that type.
A simple function for those who just can't make up their mind of what to wear, or just those who are "feeling lucky".

Explore:

The "Explore" Page is a fun way to see what outfits others have made and chosen to share publically with all Fashionkilla users.
To submit an outfit to the explore page, simply navigate to the Explore page and click the "ADD OUTFIT" button.
This will then allow you to choose an outfit to submit to the Explore page.
